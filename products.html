    PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

    Petcom offers a range of petroleum products such as E10 87 octane, E10 90 octane, automotive diesel oil, Ultra Low Sulphur Diesel and lubricants to both retail and commercial customers.

    Butane

    This is a Hydrocarbon and a highly flammable, colourless, odourless, easily liquefied gas. It is typically used as fuel for cigarette lighters and portable stoves, a propellant in aerosols, a heating fuel, a refrigerant and in the manufacture of a wide range of products.

    Propane

    This is sold to Home, School, Hotels, Restaurants and Government Facilities who utilize gas on a large scale.

    Unleaded 90

    This is for cars as well as when mixed with lubricant can be used for boats.

    Auto Diesel Oil (ADO)

    Motor oil is a lubricant used in internal combustion engines which power cars, motorcycles, lawnmowers, engine – generators, and many other machines.

    Low Sulphur Diesel

    This is a cleaner – burning diesel fuel that contains 97% less sulphur than low sulphur diesel (LSDD)

    Heavy Fuel Oil (HFO)

    This is mainly and widely used as marine fuel for low and medium speed diesel engines.

    Kerosene

    A chemical mixture derived from petroleum that is a commonly used product in Jamaican households.
